Why flomily?

flomily stands for flow + family — because managing appointments should flow, not drown in chaos.

The idea was born from everyday madness: three kids, five clubs, school notices in backpacks, practice times in WhatsApp groups, doctor's appointments over the phone. And always the same question at the end: "Is that in the calendar?"

"Managing a family is complex enough — don't let your appointments add to the stress."

So I started building a solution. First just for myself — a little helper that reads messages, finds appointments, and simply puts them in my calendar. No typing, no forgetting.

And then it grew: meal plans, automatically recognized. A waste collection calendar that reminds you to take out the bins. Game schedules from the club landing straight in the calendar. And that's just the beginning — there's more I can't tell you about yet.

Getting appointments into your existing calendar, quickly and easily — that's the core of flomily. I believe this is a real help for many people. Everything else that we already use in our family can follow step by step.

Frequently asked questions

Are appointments automatically added to my calendar?

No. flomily recognizes appointments and shows them to you for review — with all details like date, time and location. Only when you tap "Add" will you receive a regular calendar invitation via email — just like from a colleague in Outlook. You can accept, decline or edit it, just like you're used to. Nothing happens without your confirmation.

Can someone access my calendar through flomily?

No. flomily sends appointments exclusively as calendar invitations via email — just like when a colleague sends you an Outlook meeting. flomily has no access to your existing calendar and can neither read nor modify appointments.

How secure is my data?

Your messages are transmitted encrypted and only processed for appointment recognition. We don't share data with third parties for advertising. You decide which appointments are saved — and can request deletion of your data at any time. All details in our privacy policy.

Is flomily in the App Store?

No — and that's intentional. flomily runs directly in Telegram, which you probably already have. No download, no app store, no installation. You can add flomily to your home screen like an app though — then it's one tap away.

Do I have to enter each appointment individually?

No! You can send a photo of a school notice, letter, or poster — flomily recognizes all appointments on it and creates them individually. Also works with PDFs, voice messages, and forwarded texts.

Can multiple family members use flomily?

Currently each user receives their own appointments via email. Shared family calendars and inviting others to appointments are coming soon — that's one of our next features.

What does flomily cost?

flomily is currently free during the beta phase.

What are credits for?

Credits are your balance at flomily — 100 to start, +25 for each referral. The more you collect, the more you can unlock — from appointments to exclusive extras.

Does flomily work with TimeTree?

flomily and TimeTree follow different approaches: TimeTree is its own calendar where everyone has to migrate. flomily is not a calendar — it puts appointments in the calendar you already use (Outlook, Google, Apple, GMX). No migration, no second system, no lock-in.

TimeTree currently doesn't support ICS import. A direct integration is planned so appointments can also arrive in shared TimeTree calendars.

Does flomily work in other time zones?

Yes. flomily automatically detects time zones and creates appointments in the correct local time — whether you're in Berlin, London, or New York.

What commands are available in the bot?

You can use these commands anytime in the chat with the flomily bot:

  • /help — Overview of all commands
  • /credits — Show your available credits
  • /referral — Invite friends and earn credits
  • /stop — Leave flomily
